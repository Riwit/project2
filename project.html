Introduction naruto anime

Naruto is a Japanese manga series and anime television series about a young ninja named Naruto Uzumaki who dreams of becoming the leader of his village, the Hokage

Naruto Uzumaki

Naruto Uzumaki is the main character in the Naruto anime and manga series. He is introduced as a 12-year-old orphan with spiky blond hair and blue eyes. Naruto is shunned by the Hidden Leaf Village during his early life and seeks to become the village's Hokage to gain respect and prove himself.

sasuke uchiha

Sasuke Uchiha is a fictional character in the Naruto manga and anime series, created by Masashi Kishimoto. He first appears in chapter 3 of the manga, titled "Enter Sasuke!".

itachi uchiha

Itachi is first introduced as a villain in the manga in March 2003 and the anime in April 2004. He is Sasuke Uchiha's older brother and is portrayed as a terrorist working for Akatsuki.

kakashi

Kakashi is the son of Sakumo Hatake, one of the most powerful shinobi Konoha ever produced, renowned throughout the ninja world as "White Fang of the Leaf". Kakashi was himself a child prodigy and, during the ninja academy years, was hailed as the best of his generation.
